Title: Diptych

About: Pothos plant placed on top of books on music stand. Positive and negative charcoal fills. Incense also included in positive fill.

Reflection: I adjusted my placements and composition improving as I went. I was quite the perfecitonist with these pieces, taking longer than necessary and focusing on being exactly true to life. Although I am proud of the outcome, I enjoy seeing the loss of perfectionistic behavior throughout this course and the more free, interpretive linework.
