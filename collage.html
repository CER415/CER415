Title: Collage

About: Four pieces with different linework depicting important facets of personality and past. Pencil on paper.

Reflection: These are possibly the pieces I'm most proud of. I learned new hatching techniques which improved my understanding of structure and light. These pieces were the first ones where I felt I captured something emotional and personal in my work, and I will carry that lesson with me throughout my future work.
