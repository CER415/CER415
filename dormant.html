Title: Dormant

About: Violin on a stool in front of a music stand by a window partially covered by a curtain. The bow rests on the music stand. The sheet music itself is Samuel Barber's Adagio for Strings in G minor. Charcoal subtractive on paper.

Reflection: This was meant to be a moody scene-noir type of piece, very atmospheric and mysterious in nature. While I didn't exactly capture that, I do think I captured a restful, forgotten, and mysterious atmosphere with the residual graininess of the subtracted charcoal. I have played violin for ten years but stepped away during my first year of college. This resulted in a loss of community and I had to relearn a lot when I picked it back up. I wanted this piece to capture that dormant period, that feeling of waiting and familiarity that violin brings for me. The reference to "Adagio" captures some sadness and beauty that playing brings for those who are familiar with orchestral music.
