Title: Rumination/Innovation

About: Books in black and white (Rumination) and paint containers in red and gold (Innovation). Pastel on paper.

Reflection: I wish I would have chosen a subject I was more passionate about, but I'm still pleased with the result. More than anything this was an experiment with a new medium, paper, and linework. I'm pleased with how it turned out given the novelty of the elements of this work.
