Title: En préparation

About: Depiction of a fencer getting ready for competition in the style of Edgar Degas. Charcoal subtractive on paper.

Reflection: This was my final piece for the class. Although I don't think I captured the "self portrait" prompt, I am incredibly proud of this piece. It conveys a murkiness and stillness that the focus before a bout brings. I loved experimenting with linework, researching styles, and being intentional with my marks. It was also an incredibly fun piece to make, playing with light, texture, and shadow along with different levels of structure in the figure.
